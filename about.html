About us

Velvet Quill is a luxury, full-service wedding invitation and stationery studio specializing in custom, handmade creations crafted with exquisite attention to detail. Using a thoughtful blend of mixed media, we make magic.

We work with clients across the globe, offering a truly bespoke experience tailored to each couple's vision. Every piece we create is a handcrafted heirloom.

Once a client partners with us, we guide them through every detail—from color palettes to custom design elements—crafting unique invitations tailored to their vision. We work with a variety of materials, including paper, leather, wax, ribbon, velvet, and more, to bring each creation to life.
